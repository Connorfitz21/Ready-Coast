SCORING METHOD

A transparent, evidence-informed score

The score is a planning aid, not a prediction of survival and not a scientifically validated clinical or risk instrument. It converts established public preparedness guidance into visible household actions.

20 pointsWaterThree-day minimum at one gallon per person per day.
15 pointsFoodThree days of nonperishable meals.
15 pointsMedical & safetyFirst aid, medications planning, and individual needs.
10 pointsCommunicationContacts, meeting locations, and warning access.
10 pointsPower & lightingLighting, charging, and outage planning.
10 pointsEvacuationDestinations, routes, transportation, and go-bags.
10 pointsDocuments & cashCritical records and accessible emergency funds.
5 pointsHazard actionsActions selected for hazards relevant to the household.
5 pointsHousehold needsPets, accessibility, medical needs, and utilities reviewed.

The app awards points only for information or supplies the user records. It does not verify condition, accessibility, training, or real-world performance.
