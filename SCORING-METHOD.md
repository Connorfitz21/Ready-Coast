# Readiness scoring method

The Ready Coast score is an evidence-informed planning aid, not a validated prediction of survival or disaster outcome.

## 100-point allocation

- Water: 20
- Food: 15
- Medical and safety: 15
- Communication: 10
- Power and lighting: 10
- Evacuation: 10
- Documents and cash: 10
- Hazard-specific actions: 5
- Household needs: 5

The water baseline is three days at one gallon per person per day. The food baseline is three meals per person per day for three days. These are minimum planning baselines rather than guarantees of adequacy for every person, climate, or incident.

Primary public guidance used:
- CDC emergency water storage guidance
- FEMA Ready.gov planning, kit, hazard, and critical-document guidance
- American Red Cross emergency supply guidance
